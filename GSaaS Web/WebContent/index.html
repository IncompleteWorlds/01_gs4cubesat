About

Who we are

We are a new company focused on providing mission control systems for small satellite and cubesat based missions, and not so small (we hope).

The company was funded by a set of highly skilled engineers with a huge experience in the development of ground segment systems.



What we do

Developing a space mission is a difficult and complex task that requires a great effort and knowledge. We have done it for many years and for many clients, so we know how to do it very well.

Our cloud platform provides you with all the required systems for controlling your small satellite or cubesat. It will free you from the development of complex SW systems. You will only have to concentrate on your objectives.

Flexible pricing policy

We have two passions; systems development and the space. So, why not to mix both things and create the first cloud-based ground segment.

But, we do not only develop SW, we create great products that meet the expectations of our clients and help them to achieve their objectives.



Where we are

We are based on UK and Spain. We have teams in both locations



Our Great Products

CubeGS. Ground Segment as a Service

All the tools you need to monitor and control your mission in just one place

Tools included:

- Flight Dynamcis System (FDS)

- Mission Control System (MCS)

- Mission Planning System (MPS)

- Ground Station Control System (G/S CS). The network of G/S is provided by

Several communications protocols are available, like CCSDS, CSP, KISS, etc.

Customisation of the Ground Stations and bands: UHF, VHF, S-Band, etc

Total access to your Telemetry and Telecommand

In the future, Telemetry could be saved in Google Drive, Dropbox, retrieved by SFTP, etc.
